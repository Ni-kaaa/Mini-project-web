Our Wide Kitchen

Step into a kitchen where cooking becomes a delightful experience. Our wide and clean kitchen is designed to inspire culinary creativity and provide all the amenities you need for a convenient and enjoyable stay.

  • Stainless Steel Appliances: Includes a large refrigerator, stove, oven, microwave, and dishwasher, all in excellent condition for your cooking needs.
  • Small Appliances: Toaster, coffee maker, and blender are also provided, making it easy to prepare a variety of dishes and beverages.
  • Bright and Airy: Large windows fill the kitchen with natural light, creating a pleasant and uplifting environment.
  • Excellent Ventilation: A powerful range hood and windows ensure good airflow, keeping the kitchen fresh and comfortable.
